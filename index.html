LIGHT tokens are produced by the Chaos scene, maximum daily output is 100,000. LIGHT can be converted to bLIGHT through EMIT's cross-chain bridge. No ICO.

DARK tokens are produced by the Chaos scene, maximum daily output is 100,000. DARK can be converted to bDARK through EMIT's cross-chain bridge. No ICO.
